## ðŸš€ Getting Started

### Playing as a Guest
1. **Open the game** in your browser
2. **Enter your name** in the player name field
3. **Choose a game mode**:
   - Click **"Quick Play"** for instant game with bots (casual mode)
   - Click **"Create Game"** to start your own game room
   - Click **"Join Game"** to join an existing game with a code

### Creating an Account
1. **Click "Register"** in the top-right corner of the lobby
2. **Fill in the registration form**:
   - Username (3-50 characters)
   - Email address (for account recovery)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Display name (optional)
3. **Click "Create Account"**
4. **Check your email** for verification link
5. **Click the verification link** to activate your account

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: Can I play without creating an account?**
A: Yes! You can play as a guest with any name. However, you won't have access to stats tracking, achievements, or social features.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login screen and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link.

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Currently, usernames cannot be changed after registration. Choose carefully!

### Gameplay

**Q: What's the difference between Casual and Ranked?**
A: Casual games don't affect your permanent statistics or leaderboard ranking. Ranked games count toward your profile stats and achievements.

**Q: How do I play with friends?**
A: Create a game and share the 6-character game code with your friends. They can join using this code.

**Q: Can I rejoin if I disconnect?**
A: Yes! The game saves your session. Just return to the lobby and you'll see a "Rejoin Game" option.

### Features

**Q: How do I unlock achievements?**
A: Achievements unlock automatically as you play. Check the Achievements panel to see requirements and track progress.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**
A: Yes! The game is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. A dedicated app is coming soon.

**Q: How do I report bugs or suggest features?**
A: Use the feedback button in the settings menu or contact support through the website.

### Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Modern versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: Why is my connection poor?**
A: Check your internet connection. The game uses WebSocket connections which may be blocked by some firewalls.

**Q: How do I clear my data?**
A: Go to Settings > Advanced > Clear Cache. Note: This will log you out and clear local preferences.
